What is this organization about?

Wild welfare continually excel in their provision of expertise to improve the welfare of captive wild animals, developing improvements which have a positive impact on all animals within facilities they work with, and in the development of positive working relationships with government officials and regional zoo associations. Wild Welfare is vital to ensuring that the global zoo and aquarium community focuses on positive animal welfare in all circumstances, in all countries.

Conservation Efforts

They have partnered with many other organizations as well as had major donors to use towards saving the Arctic Wolves. They have many research facilities and provide animal care and management. They work directly with zoo's, rescues, and aquariums in order to give back to the wildlife.

What can you do in order to help with is foundation?

They have various ways in order for you to donate and assist with providing equipment and medical supplies they can use towards the animals. You can also take it into your own hands and volunteer at shelters to ensure that the animals are taken great care of. If you are interested in helping with this cause then please visit the Wild Welfare Organization and consider donating to help other bring back the rejuvenating life of Arctic Wolves.
